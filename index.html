AI-Powered Vanilla Orchid Pollination Is Coming Soon!

We're developing the world's first AI-driven vanilla orchid pollination robot—designed to help farmers automate one of agriculture’s most time-consuming tasks.

Vanilla is one of the most valuable crops in the world, yet each flower must be hand-pollinated in just a day. Due to cheap labor, we've had to rely on places like madigascar for 80% of our vanilla.

Our mission at AIFarmed is to change that and make it possible to grow in the US, as well as cheaper pollination abroad!
